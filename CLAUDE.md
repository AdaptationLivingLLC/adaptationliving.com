### Color System
- Dark navy: #0B1D3A (headers, hero, footer, CTA blocks)
- Cream gradient: linear-gradient(165deg, #FEFCF8, #FDF6EC, #FAF0E0, #FEFCF8) (content sections)
- Gold: #D4AF37 (accent, CTAs, highlights, borders)
- Text on dark: #ffffff (main), #B8C4D4 (muted), #8899AA (dim)
- Text on cream: #0B1D3A (main), #4A5568 (muted), #718096 (dim)

### Typography
- Display: Cinzel (serif, 400/600/700)
- Body: Inter (sans-serif, 300/400/600/700)

### Design Rules
- No full-screen sections (no min-h-screen, h-screen, 100vh)
- Mostly cream/light backgrounds — dark navy only for hero, video section, final CTA
- Everything easy to read
- Symmetrical layouts (4-card 2x2 grids, not 3-card awkward wraps)
- Smaller text sizes — nothing oversized
- Gold gradient dividers between sections
- Navbar: transparent → solid navy on scroll with gold border
